About us:

We are a small family business that started in the early '70s, serving the highest quality and freshest seafood possible. Since then, we have become a well-known restaurant on the island with many loyal customers visiting.


In our restaurant, we try to keep the island's atmosphere to make you truly feel on a Mexican island in the Caribbean, with a setting perfect for romantic occasions or the whole family. Nevertheless, every day we do our best to offer the best possible experience to our customers, constantly trying to improve in every way.
Sincerely, your host, Enrique.
